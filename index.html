About me

Having grown up in the Bay Area, I have always had a strong inclination toward creativity and what can be expressed though different mediums. From a very early age, I found visual arts and writing, which I often rushed home to work on from school. Recently I have discovered programming, and the medium of code. Just like an eloquent sentence or a stunning painting, I've found succinct code to have an appealing draw, and find its structure under appreciated.

My discoveries lead me to find web development as a transfixing union between all of my interests. Currently I am in a coding bootcamp learning full stack web development, while also utilizing outside resources to increase my knowledge. Web development and programming is a field which allows and rewards perpetual curiosity, and I am very excited where these pursuits will take me.
